02 / About

Unfelt is a home for artists who don't want to sound like anyone else.

Unfelt is an independent electronic music studio founded by Natan Keller and Jamerson Costa. We work with music production, co-production, composition, mixing and mastering, exclusive vocals, sound design and tools for producers.

Unfelt was born from the idea of finding something that has not been felt yet: music with atmosphere, texture and intention, not built from the same formulas. Our work moves through different sides of electronic music, always focused on helping artists and projects find a sound that feels like their own.
